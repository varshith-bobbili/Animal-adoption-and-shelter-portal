About Our Shelter

At Paws & Tails, we're dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming animals in need. Our shelter provides a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals, giving them a second chance at a happy life.

Since our founding, we've helped over 1,000 animals find their forever homes. Join us in our mission to create a world where every pet has a loving home.
